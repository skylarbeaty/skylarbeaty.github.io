Procedural Generation

I’ve created several different methods of procedurally generating levels, including custom solutions for my own projects, like I did for Phase Drift. I’ve also recreated methods from popular games, like Spelunky and The Binding of Isaac, in order to have a template for making tutorials on the subject.

The code for these can be found on my GitHub page, and the tutorials are on my Youtube page.

About Me

The Basics

I'm William Beaty, a programmer completing their Bachelors of Computer Science. Creator of many games, some of which are even good :) I’ve worn a lot of hats in game dev. The ones that fit most comfortably are game design and game programming. My favorite times creating games have been sharing that experience with a team, and creating something together that is greater than what any of us could do alone.

I like to build, upgrade and tinker with computers. I get some kind of strange enjoyment out of learning new tools. It makes me more powerful ^.^ When I can steal myself away from the computer, and I’m not otherwise engaged, I listen to music or attempt to play it. I like to sew some of my own garments. I’m interested in space flight and I’ve been keeping up with recent launches and watching live when I can, as well as learning a lot about the technology involved.
